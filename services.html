The Services We Offer!

Below you will see some of the amazing services we offer here at Bonnie Farm Park!

Local Produce Shop

Here at Bonnie Farm Park we grow and sell our very own delcicious produce ranging from fruits like strawberries, apples and pears aswell as Vegetables such as Potatoes, Asparagus, Corn and We even sell plants. The prices may vary depending on what you want and how much you want but always know when you buy Fresh delicious produce from Bonnie Farm Park we will always give you the best prices possible.

Food & Drinks

We offer a takeaway food service across the site, with indoor seating available in the Bonnie cafe and plenty of outdoor undercover seating across the Park with a lovely view. All dietaries are catered for in our seasonal menu.

Animal Feeding Activity

During your visit you will be able to buy some food from the Cafe for the animals In the feeding barn which is a great way for kids and adults alike to bond with the animals.

Kids Play Park

For whenever your kids get a little bored waiting for their parents to finish their food at the cafe there is a Play park just outside the cafe which includes Swings, Monkey bars, toddlers play area, Sandpit and a Zipline!

Petting Zoo

Every afternoon, under the supervision of our staff, children and adults have the opportunity to handle some of our smaller friends. You can handle and feed our: Rabbits, Mice, Guinea Pigs, Turtles, Baby Lambs and more.
