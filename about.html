Our Mission

To provide a fun outdoor learning environment for children and families within Scotland. We create opportunities for all children and families to learn and actively participate in horse riding, farming, agriculture and animal care aswell as providing everyone with opportunities to buy our amazing produce.

Our Objective

To spark interest and engage the next generation in horse riding, farming and agriculture. To provide a fun outdoor setting that will encourage people to become more active and maybe even persue a career.

Our Vision

To see children and families more active and engaged in the outdoors and regenerate interest in young farming and animal care

Opening Times

Our opening times are as follows, 7:30am to 6:00pm from Monday to Friday and our park is open all year round except for the Christmas Break.
